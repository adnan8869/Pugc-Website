Who We Are

It is for the first time in 124-year history of the Punjab University that it has established a teaching campus outside Lahore. Gujranwala Campus has been established with the keen interest & support of the Honourable & Worthy Vice-Chancellor.

The opening of the sub-campus is in accordance with the educational policy of the present regime of President Islamic Republic of Pakistan, who is taking a keen interest in promoting education. Gujranwala campus is located near Alipur Chowk, Rawalpindi By-pass. It has an area of 81 Kanals. It has 37 classrooms, 1 Administration block, fully air-conditioned and open shelves library, 1 big multi-purpose hall and allied facilities i.e., Car Parking, Canteen, Book Shop, and Grassy Lawns. It provides a vibrant academic environment, ensuring the holistic development of students in an inspiring and well-equipped campus.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to provide high-quality education that fosters academic excellence, critical thinking, and professional skills. We are committed to developing well-rounded individuals who are equipped to meet the challenges of a dynamic world, contributing positively to society and the nation.

Our vision is to become a leading center of education, recognized for academic innovation, research, and community engagement. We aspire to cultivate a learning environment that encourages intellectual growth, creativity, and social responsibility, shaping the future leaders of tomorrow.
